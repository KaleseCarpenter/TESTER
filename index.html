About Me

I am from northern Ohio.

I enjoy going to live shows.

I want to travel the wotld for a living, own an organic farm, buy a boat and have a home right in front of the beach.

It has always been a dream of mine to to learn coding languages so that I can develop apps and explore different avenues within the music industry.

I was introduced to HTML by Myspace and it motivated me to go to college in San Francisco for Web Design and New Media / Music Production and Sound.

I am on a journey to become the highest version of myself. I am falling in love with my journey. I look forward to financial freedom and having more time to spend with family and friends.
